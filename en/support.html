Our promise

Your donation arrives in full.

Administration, travel, postage – we cover these from our own pockets or from membership fees. Donations flow undiminished into projects and scholarships.

Every two to three years we travel to Tanzania to confirm progress in person. The flight costs are generally borne by the travellers themselves.
