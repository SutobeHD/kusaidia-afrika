4. Your rights

You have the right at any time to obtain free information about the origin, recipients and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have the right to request rectification or deletion of this data. If you have given consent to processing, you may withdraw it at any time for future use. You also have the right to request restriction of processing and to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.

5. SSL / TLS encryption

For security reasons, this site uses SSL / TLS encryption. An encrypted connection is recognised by "https://" in the address bar and the lock icon in your browser.

6. Contact form & e-mail

If you contact us through the form or by e-mail / phone / fax, your details (including any contact information) will be stored to process your request and for follow-up. We do not share this data without your consent.

7. Analytics

This website uses WP Statistics – a privacy-friendly analytics tool. Data is stored exclusively on our own server, and your IP address is anonymised so it cannot be linked to you directly.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files that do not damage your device. They are either temporarily stored for the duration of a session (session cookies) or permanently (persistent cookies). Session cookies are automatically deleted at the end of your visit.

9. Embedded third-party content

This site may embed YouTube videos (operator: Google Ireland Ltd., Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland) and Google Maps. When you load such content, data is transmitted to Google's servers, which may be located in the USA. See policies.google.com/privacy for details.
