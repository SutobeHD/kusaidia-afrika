July 2024 · Auction

After-sale of the charity art auction

The art auction on Saturday, 15 June 2024 was a great success.

Some works were not sold under the hammer. These are now available at the limit price. For some sold works, second copies remain available – offered at the price achieved during the auction.

For purchase inquiries please contact rolf.schnee@web.de or kontakt@kusaidia-afrika.de.

October 2023 · Project update

Clinical Medicine course launched

In early August 2023, the state regulatory authority approved the launch of the new Clinical Medicine course at the Bishop Nicodemus Hhando College of Health Science. The first cohort started in October 2023 – with laptops provided by Kusaidia Afrika.
